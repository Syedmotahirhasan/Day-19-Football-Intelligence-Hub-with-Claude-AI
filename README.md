# Day 19 — Football Intelligence Hub with Claude AI

## Task Overview
Built a complete Football Intelligence Hub using Claude AI by uploading 
a FIFA World Cup Excel workbook and generating personalized football insights.

## Steps Completed
1. Downloaded the football workbook from Google Drive
2. Opened Claude and set effort level to Low
3. Uploaded the football Excel workbook to Claude
4. Pasted the Football Intelligence Hub prompt
5. Answered the Football Knowledge Level Check (5 questions)
6. Reviewed the FIFA World Cup 2026 Prediction Report
7. Completed the Football IQ Quiz (10 questions, Easy to Expert)
8. Reviewed my Football Awareness Score
9. Completed the Messi vs Ronaldo Personality Match assessment
10. Reviewed my Football Personality Archetype
11. Generated my final Football Intelligence Profile & FIIQ Report Card
12. Took screenshots and saved results

## Outputs Generated
- Knowledge Level: Knowledgeable Fan (76/100)
- Football IQ: Smart Fan (70/100)
- Awareness Score: 72/100
- Overall FIIQ Score: 74/100
- Personality Archetype: Balanced Maestro (leaning Messi)
- WC 2026 Predicted Winner: Argentina
